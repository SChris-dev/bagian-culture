Batik Jawa

Batik is a distinctively patterned fabric crafted by applying wax with a canting and processed traditionally. It involves creating patterns on mori or colored woven cloth, resulting in functional clothing. The term "batik" from Javanese signifies the art of adorning fabric with wax for unique patterns and color fields, reflecting meticulous and traditional craftsmanship.
